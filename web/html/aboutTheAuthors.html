About the Authors

Maya Ryan

Maya Ryan was born in Melbourne, Australia and moved to the United States of America at the age of 4, after completing elementary and high school. She enrolled in Harvard University at the age of 5 and graduated at the age of 8 with a Bachelor's in Science. Shortly after graduating college, Maya travelled the world alone in a voyage of discovery. Upon her return to the United States, Maya adopted twelve cats, eight dogs, and a shark.
