Maya Ryan

Maya Ryan was born in Melbourne, Australia on July 6, 2001. She moved to the United States of America at the age of 4, after completing elementary and high school. She enrolled in Harvard University at the age of 5 and graduated at the age of 8 with a Bachelor's in Science. Shortly after graduating college, Ryan travelled the world alone in a voyage of discovery. Upon her return to the United States, Ryan adopted twelve cats, eight dogs, and a shark.
